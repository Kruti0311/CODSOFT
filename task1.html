Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

[31 October 1875-15 December 1950]

>

Vallabhbhai Patel was a senior leader of the Indian National Congress and a prominentfigure in the Indian Freedom Struggle, who later became India’s first Deputy Prime Minister and first Home Minister.

Let's see the biography of Sardar Patel

  • Vallabhbhai Patel was born in Nadiad, Gujarat, on October 31, 1875 (His birth anniversary is now observed as National Unity Day or Rashtriya Ekta Diwas).

  • He hailed from a farmer’s family. In his early years, Patel was considered by many as an unambitious man destined for a commonplace job.

  • He passed law examination, often studying himself, with borrowed books.

  • Patel practised law at Godhra, Borsad, and Anand in Gujarat, after passing the bar examination.

  • He earned the reputation of being a fierce and skilled lawyer.
